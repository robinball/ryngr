How to Forward Your Calls

Forward unanswered calls from your existing number to your Ryngr number so we can send auto-replies.

  1. iPhone: Go to Settings → Phone → Call Forwarding and enter your Ryngr number. Or dial *61*# to forward only unanswered calls.
  2. Android: Open Phone app → Settings → Call Forwarding → Forward when unanswered and enter your Ryngr number.
  3. Landline / VoIP: Contact your provider and ask them to set up "call forward on no answer" to .
  4. Test it: Call your business number and let it ring. You should receive an auto-SMS within seconds!
