FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How does Ryngr know when I miss a call?
You set up call forwarding on your business line so that unanswered calls are routed to your dedicated Ryngr number. When a call comes in and isn't answered, Ryngr automatically sends the caller an SMS.
Do I need to install any apps?
Nope! Ryngr works entirely through call forwarding and SMS — there's nothing to install. You manage everything from the web dashboard.
Can I customize the SMS message?
Absolutely. During onboarding you write your own custom SMS template. Include your name, business info, and booking link — make it sound exactly like you.
What if the caller replies to the SMS?
Replies are forwarded directly to your WhatsApp so you can continue the conversation naturally, without revealing your personal phone number.
Is there a contract or commitment?
No contracts, no hidden fees. It's $39/month and you can cancel anytime from your settings page. We also offer a free trial so you can try it risk-free.
What kind of phone number do I get?
You get a real local phone number with your area code so it looks familiar and trustworthy to your callers.
